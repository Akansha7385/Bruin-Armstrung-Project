HOW TO BUY

CREATE A WALLET

Download Metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or Google play for free Desktop users, download the Google Chrome extension by going to Metamask.

GET SOME ETH

Have ETH in your wallet to switch to $BRIUN. If you don't have any ETH, you can buy directly on Metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

GO TO UNISWAP

Connect to Uniswap. Go to Uniswap to google chrome or on the browser inside your Metamask app.Connect your wallet. Paste the $BRIUN token address into Uniswap, select $BRIUN, and confirm. When Metamask prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.
